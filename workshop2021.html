Student Workshop 2021

Note: This page is currently under construction. Check back here for updates.

The 2021 CMU HoTT Student Workshop is a meeting of graduate students affiliated with the Homotopy Type Theory group at CMU, to exchange ideas related to homotopy type theory, logic, and category theory. The workshop will consist of a series of presentations, with opportunities for discussion. All are welcome to attend; if you are interested in speaking, please contact Jonas Frey. This event is meant to be friendly and informal, and talks on work in progress and unfinished ideas are welcome.

  • When: Friday, 26 February 2021, 10am - 4:30pm (tentatively — times subject to change)
  • Where: Remotely (zoom link to be announced)
